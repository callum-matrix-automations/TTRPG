This has meant that only Opus is able to handle the massive size of the prompts, but even that is weak because it can't do everything at once.

- Tracking Equipment/outfits (Inventory is practically impossible to maintain consistently)
- Tracking Tiers/State of character
- Tracking Stats, resources, skills, abilities and status effects.
- Tracking Quests & hints
- Act as a GM for the entire RP
- Create an interesting narrative with plot twists and story beats
